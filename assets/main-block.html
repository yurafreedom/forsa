Advantages05
New era of
decentralization

Now decentralization is achieved not only by the equality of an unlimited number of nodes, but also by the equality of blockchains within a single network, with cross-blockchain transactions.

Fast as light

Brand new network Protocol, in a new way organizing and structuring the communication of nodes among themselves as well as rethinking the architecture of data transmission within the blockchain.

Ready for mass
adoption

All DApss are stored on blockchain and are easily accessible to anyone on the web. Without commissions for transactions sending valuables and information is available as never before.

Gathered all previous blockchain features

Protection from Double Spending and DDoS attacks, Attack- and censorship-resistant, creating tokens and smart-contracts, hight performance, true distributed network and data storage and more.

Development of DApps

Easy Mining

Anyone can become a full node and start mining. The application is completely free and easy to install. It is not necessary to download the entire blockchain.
